Active vs Passive Weight Changes

How it works: Compares two consecutive quarters. If the overall portfolio weight ratio changed (e.g. top 10 went from 60% to 65%), each stock's weight is expected to shift proportionally — that's the passive/market component. Any deviation from that expected weight is classified as active trading (the manager bought or sold). New entries and exits are always active decisions.
